How to use Smart Lot

Smart Lot allows you to find parking without the hassle of driving up and down aisles in a lot.
Simply open the app, select a parking lot near you, and you will be given a bird's eye view of the parking lot from your phone.
Red spots indicate that the parking spot is taken and green spots indicate available parking.
Certain spots may be designated different colors depending on the location such as handicap spots or other reserved spots.
